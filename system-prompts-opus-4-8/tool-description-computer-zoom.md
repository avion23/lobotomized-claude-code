  Describes the computer-use zoom tool for taking read-only higher-resolution
  screenshots of regions

Take a higher-resolution screenshot of a specific region of the last full-screen screenshot, to inspect small text, button labels, or fine UI details hard to read in the downsampled image. Coordinates in subsequent click calls always refer to the full-screen screenshot, not the zoomed image.
